Booking & Reservations

Reservation Process

All bookings are subject to availability and must be made through our official website, mobile application, or authorized booking channels. Provisional bookings will be held for 24 hours only.

Eligibility

Girls Girls Hostel is exclusively for female students and working professionals aged 18 years and above. Valid government-issued photo ID proof is mandatory at the time of check-in.

Booking Confirmation

Your booking is confirmed only after:

  • Receipt of complete booking form with accurate information
  • Successful payment of security deposit and first month's rent
  • Verification of submitted documents
  • Issuance of confirmation email with booking reference number

Important: Bookings made with false information will be considered invalid and will be cancelled without refund.

Payment Terms

Fee Structure

Our fee structure includes:

  • Monthly accommodation charges (varies by room type)
  • Security deposit (refundable at the time of checkout)
  • Maintenance charges (if applicable)
  • Any additional services availed

Payment Schedule

Rent is payable monthly in advance, due on or before the 5th of each month. Late payments will attract a penalty of â‚¹100 per day after the due date.

Security Deposit

A security deposit equivalent to one month's rent is required at the time of booking. This deposit is refundable upon checkout, subject to:

  • No damage to hostel property
  • Clearance of all outstanding dues
  • Proper handover of room and belongings
  • Minimum 30-day notice period served

Note: Security deposit refunds will be processed within 15 working days from the date of checkout and final settlement.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellation by Resident

Cancellation requests must be submitted in writing via email or through our official portal. Refund eligibility is as follows:

  • More than 30 days before check-in: Full refund of security deposit and advance rent
  • 15-30 days before check-in: 50% refund of security deposit, full refund of advance rent
  • Less than 15 days before check-in: No refund of security deposit, 50% refund of advance rent
  • After check-in: Security deposit forfeited, rent calculated on pro-rata basis

Cancellation by Hostel

We reserve the right to cancel a booking under exceptional circumstances including:

  • Provision of false information during booking
  • Violation of hostel rules and code of conduct
  • Non-payment of dues
  • Emergency situations requiring hostel closure

In such cases, full refund will be provided for unused accommodation.

Check-in & Check-out

Check-in Procedure

Standard check-in time is 12:00 PM. Early check-in may be available subject to room availability and may incur additional charges.

Required documents at check-in:

  • Government-issued photo ID (Aadhar Card, Passport, or Driver's License)
  • Student ID card (if applicable)
  • Passport-size photographs (2 copies)
  • Booking confirmation receipt

Check-out Procedure

Standard check-out time is 11:00 AM. Late check-out may be permitted until 2:00 PM with prior approval and may incur half-day charges.

Check-out process includes:

  • Room inspection by hostel management
  • Settlement of any outstanding dues
  • Return of hostel property (keys, access cards, etc.)
  • Security deposit refund processing

Code of Conduct

General Behavior

All residents are expected to maintain decorum and respect the rights of fellow residents. Any form of harassment, discrimination, or disruptive behavior will not be tolerated.

Visitors Policy

For security reasons, male visitors are not permitted in rooms or common areas. Female visitors are allowed in common areas between 8:00 AM and 8:00 PM only, with prior registration at the reception.

Quiet Hours

Quiet hours are observed from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM. During this time, residents are expected to maintain silence in corridors and rooms to ensure peaceful environment for all.

Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited within hostel premises:

  • Smoking, consumption of alcohol, or any illegal substances
  • Gambling or any form of betting
  • Possession of weapons or hazardous materials
  • Commercial activities without prior permission
  • Tampering with security systems or CCTV cameras

Important: Violation of code of conduct may result in immediate termination of accommodation without refund.

Facilities Usage

Common Areas

Common areas including lounge, study room, and dining area are shared facilities. Residents are expected to maintain cleanliness and use these areas responsibly.

Kitchen & Dining

The common kitchen is available for light cooking between 6:00 AM and 10:00 PM. Residents must clean up after use and follow all safety guidelines.

Laundry Facilities

Washing machines are available for use as per the scheduled timings. Residents must provide their own detergent and follow operating instructions carefully.

Wi-Fi & Internet

Complimentary Wi-Fi is provided for educational and personal use. Downloading copyrighted material, accessing inappropriate content, or any illegal online activity is strictly prohibited.

Liability & Security

Personal Belongings

Residents are solely responsible for their personal belongings. The hostel management is not liable for any loss, theft, or damage to personal property.

Room Security

While we maintain 24/7 security with CCTV surveillance and security personnel, residents are advised to:

  • Lock their rooms when unattended
  • Not share access cards or keys with others
  • Report any suspicious activity immediately
  • Not allow unauthorized persons into the hostel

Health & Emergency

In case of medical emergency, hostel staff will provide immediate assistance and arrange for medical help. However, all medical expenses will be borne by the resident.

Emergency Contact: Please ensure your emergency contact information is always updated in our records.

Privacy Policy

Information Collection

Data Usage

  • Legal and regulatory compliance

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your data will not be shared with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.
