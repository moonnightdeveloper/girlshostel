Policy Overview

At Girls Girls Hostel, we strive to maintain transparency and fairness in all our financial transactions. This refund policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds are provided and the procedures to follow for refund requests.

General Principles

Our refund policy is based on the following principles:

  • Fair treatment of all residents and applicants
  • Transparency in refund calculations and procedures
  • Timely processing of eligible refund requests
  • Clear communication regarding refund status

Important: All refund requests must be submitted in writing via email or through our official portal. Verbal requests will not be processed.

Cancellation Refunds

Pre-Check-in Cancellations

If you need to cancel your booking before check-in, the following refund policy applies:

Cancellation Period Security Deposit Advance Rent Processing Fee
More than 30 days before check-in 100% refund 100% refund No charge
15-30 days before check-in 50% refund 100% refund ₹500
7-14 days before check-in 25% refund 75% refund ₹500
Less than 7 days before check-in No refund 50% refund ₹500

Note: The processing fee covers administrative costs associated with booking and cancellation procedures. This fee is non-refundable once the cancellation process has been initiated.

Security Deposit Refund

Refund Conditions

Your security deposit is refundable upon checkout, subject to the following conditions:

  • No damage to hostel property, furniture, or fixtures
  • All outstanding dues are cleared
  • Room is handed over in clean and orderly condition
  • All hostel property (keys, access cards, etc.) is returned
  • Minimum 30-day notice period is served before checkout
  • No pending complaints from other residents or staff

Deductions from Security Deposit

The following may result in deductions from your security deposit:

  • Damage to property beyond normal wear and tear
  • Cleaning charges if room requires extra cleaning
  • Replacement costs for lost or damaged items
  • Outstanding utility or service charges
  • Penalties for rule violations (if applicable)

Room Inspection: A joint inspection of the room will be conducted at the time of checkout in the presence of the resident. Any issues will be documented and discussed at that time.

Early Check-out Refunds

Voluntary Early Check-out

If you choose to check out before your planned departure date, the following policy applies:

Notice Period Rent Refund Security Deposit
30 days or more notice Pro-rata refund for unused days Full refund (subject to conditions)
15-29 days notice Pro-rata refund minus 15% penalty Full refund (subject to conditions)
7-14 days notice Pro-rata refund minus 30% penalty Full refund (subject to conditions)
Less than 7 days notice No rent refund Full refund (subject to conditions)

Calculation Example

If you paid ₹8,500 for a month (30 days) and check out after 15 days with 20 days notice:

  • Daily rate: ₹8,500 ÷ 30 = ₹283.33
  • Unused days: 15 days × ₹283.33 = ₹4,250
  • Penalty (15%): ₹4,250 × 0.15 = ₹637.50
  • Refund amount: ₹4,250 - ₹637.50 = ₹3,612.50

Refund Process

Step-by-Step Procedure

Follow these steps to request and receive your refund:

1

Submit Written Request

Send a formal refund request via email to refunds@Girlshostel.com or through our official portal. Include your booking reference number and reason for refund.

2

Request Verification

Our team will verify your eligibility for refund based on our policy terms and the timing of your request. We may request additional information if needed.

3

Refund Calculation

We will calculate the refund amount based on our policy, considering any applicable deductions, penalties, or processing fees.

4

Approval & Processing

Once approved, the refund will be processed through the original payment method. You will receive a confirmation email with refund details.

5

Fund Transfer

The refund amount will be transferred to your account. The time taken for the funds to reflect depends on your bank's processing time.

Processing Timeframes

Note: These timelines may vary during peak seasons or bank holidays. We appreciate your patience and understanding during the refund process.

Special Cases & Exceptions

Medical Emergencies

In case of genuine medical emergencies that prevent you from joining or continuing your stay, we offer special consideration:

  • Full refund of security deposit and advance rent with valid medical certificate
  • No processing fees applied
  • Priority processing of refund request
  • Medical certificate must be from a recognized hospital or medical practitioner

Hostel-Initiated Cancellations

If we need to cancel your booking due to unforeseen circumstances, you will receive:

  • Full refund of all amounts paid
  • Assistance in finding alternative accommodation (if requested)
  • Priority booking for future dates (if desired)

Force Majeure

In cases of force majeure (natural disasters, government restrictions, etc.):

  • Refunds will be processed as per government guidelines and our capability
  • Alternative arrangements may be offered where possible
  • Communication regarding the situation will be provided regularly

Documentation Required: For special case refunds, appropriate documentation (medical certificates, official notices, etc.) must be provided to support your request.
